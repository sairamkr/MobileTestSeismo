*By Trinity & Suhaani*

*"They're mutating."*
*"The locusts and the flies? Mutating. The ones that die and fall out of the Unsea have massive black eyes now. They're adapting."*

**Plaguegate.** The Church raved about it; the public just shrugged, uncertain. Because every time a new billionaire comes into existence, the ten plagues of Egypt cycle across the land once more.

The first time, the people of Egypt were evacuated, absorbed by the neighbouring countries as refugees. The nation's straight-ruled borders thrown into sharper relief. Then the plagues began to compound: cycles upon cycles of the same ten, over and over. A wall of near-sentient blackness, lit now and then by falling burning hail, its meagre light swallowed by swarm after swarm of locusts, chirping and buzzing, making a sound like crashing waves.

The old lifeblood of Egypt, now literally that: the river runs thick with blood, rich and red and deeply unsanitary. Though the transfusion patients don't seem to mind. (B+ is in excess already. O- would have made the ideal blood-river, but the gods are never that kind.)

**"Lo! Cust Protein is set to become one of the largest food producers in history! CEO Unneth Ikal is among the youngest billionaires alive : Forbes 40 Under 4-"**

Researchers gave up counting the plagues; they overlapped too much. Frogs, flies, all of it. They became a godsent supply of free food. **An infinite money glitch.** The Church praised Unneth and the other CEOs for showing God that even under punishment, we can make something good. Parents repeated the sentiment to their children.

One phase of the plague never quite ends before the next begins. A writhing brew of blood, lice, flies, frogs, and inky dark became the **Unsea.** The Unsea's former government fought to keep ownership of it and its near-endless resources. They lost.

The Unsea is capitalised upon, and another cycle of plagues breaks out. The border once drawn in pen on paper now looks drawn by God's own hand! Physical proof that even God endorses the ruler's line. It has become a tourist attraction, bought by **Datney.**

*"Come! You'll never unsee the Unsea! Those exposed often develop boils, lice, and death upon entry, so do NOT enter the Unsea. T&C apply."*

Conspiracy theorists rant about the Plaguegate meter: *"Billionaires are single-handedly the cause of the Unsea!"* they cry, when the swarms run especially thick. The agencies bury the real reason, a perfect Pinocchio.

In the darkness the flies, lice, and locusts form their own ecosystem, immune to the bacteria, adapting to the dark. The Unsea blocks critical trade routes, and the petroleum trapped within it is worth millions for its origin alone. Underground caverns are dug to harvest the oil, but God save anyone caught by those mutated locusts. The burning hailstones make priceless jewellery. Even God could not have imagined the ways human greed can consume.

Our planet may not be eternal, but control of the Unsea is control of the world, and the Unsea is as eternal as God. As eternal as the rich. So the billionaires stop appearing in public, to limit the plagues they trigger: *others shouldn't get richer off my investment.* Others give away just enough to stop being billionaires, 999 million, so no undeserving protein-brick brand gets rich off the next cycle.

The land's name, from before the Unsea, is wiped from the record. Only Plaguegate and the Unsea remain.

**Your family's from the Unsea? So do you have the... y'know... boils?**
