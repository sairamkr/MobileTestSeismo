*By Jeevitesh*

**The soil stopped giving around 3081\.** Not all at once but gradually, the way everything terrible happens quietly, politely, until one day the ground simply refused. 

Crops thinned to whispers. Yields became jokes. The world did not panic because a company called NUUL announced it had a solution and the solution was cheap and it worked and that was enough. A full stomach asks very few questions.

**NUUL ran the internet. Then it ran the global food supply. Nobody voted on this.** The merger was announced on a Tuesday and by Wednesday everyone had moved on because there was new content to watch and dinner was still arriving on time.

In cities with no tourist maps, in buildings with no windows and no names on the door, **NUUL hired thousands of people to watch screens for AI content moderation. Their job was to see the worst things humans do to each other every hour, every day, for years and remove it before the rest of the world saw it too** while scrolling between recipes and pet videos. The pay was almost nothing. The contract was long. They signed because there was nothing else. There is always nothing else for some people. That is not an accident.

What happened inside those rooms was not burnout. Burnout is a word for people whose suffering the world intends to eventually acknowledge. This was something else. The brain, forced to process extreme violence at industrial scale for years, does not go numb. It rewires. Empathy pathways prune themselves. Fear responses flatten completely. **The body begins producing a protein compound found nowhere else in human biology.** Only in them, did we discover a solution to our depleted soil. A protein produced under duress \- that could rejuvenate our land.

The ones who stopped watching were not allowed to stop. There were chairs built specifically for this. The ones whose bodies gave out completely were put back into the ground outside the same ground they walked on every morning. **The altered human body, NUUL confirmed, produced the most fertile organic compound ever tested.**

**The bodies fed the soil. The soil fed the crops. The crops fed the world.** The cycle was clean. The numbers were excellent. The meeting moved to the next agenda item.
