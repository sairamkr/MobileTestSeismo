Join the world's first **Zero-Waste Food Chain.**

Fertile land is shrinking. Oceans are stressed. Petroleum is outdated. But plastic? Abundant. Reliable. Loyal.

Congratulations! You're already a contributor. Every sip of water, every packaged snack, every breath of this skyline carries microplastics. They've been integrating into your body for years. You didn't even have to try.

**The Preparation**

*Step 1 Collect the "Ingredients."* We gather plastic waste from oceans, streets, and landfills, pollution reimagined as raw material, and detoxify it.
*Step 2 Add the Good Stuff.* We enrich the plastic with proteins, vitamins, and minerals until it's nutritionally balanced.
*Step 3 Print Your Meal.* The material becomes edible filament for 4D food printers that produce custom-shaped meals.

**The Afterlife**

*Step 1 Recover the "Investment."* After death, our certified Polymer Retrieval Specialists™ recover this valuable material, with a 99.9% guarantee.
*Step 2 Spin the Filaments.* The recovered polymer is refined and spun into edible filament that reacts to heat, moisture, and saliva.
*Step 3 The Return Policy.* The meals are served in restaurants across the world. Nothing is buried, burned, or wasted.

A perfectly circular system, where **everything comes back to the table, including you, and the ones you love.**
