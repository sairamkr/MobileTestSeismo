*By Vaishnavi & Priyanshi*

In a world that needs a constant stream of stateless bodies to justify its borders, a steady supply of stateless men has been a godsend.

**Now that the government has merged brothels with deportation centres (two places that already live just outside respectability) the two can feed a single loop.** Brothels lure customers, who are then promptly deported or conscripted into stateless mercenary armies.

**Apsaras** are independent recruiters working inside the brothels, hired or funded by the government or international aid and military organisations. Each time one finds a particularly adept candidate who passes through the service, an invisible transaction occurs: the customer's citizenship is seized. From that moment the man becomes stateless, a citizen of nowhere, reclassified as a **gigolo**. A creature with no rights, no protection, no legal evidence of his existence. A ghost mercenary at the mercy of his employers.

Every day the government collects thousands of these unsuspecting gigolos. Passports, birth certificates, domicile certificates, bank details, PAN cards, Aadhaar cards are all systematically destroyed.

As stateless denizens, gigolos become useful resources: to wage illegal wars and massacres, to run guns, to smuggle drugs across borders. The WHO has now authorised their use for tentative organ extraction and medical experiments; the UN puts them to extreme labour.

Since governments already run vast digital identity systems from passports, to biometric IDs, national databases, citizenship is largely digital, and deleting someone's data erases their legal existence with ease. Millions are already stateless; adding a few million more would barely register.

The unsurveilled interiors of brothels make an ideal place to conduct these secretive deportations. **Shielded from society's opprobrium and veiled from the gaze of civility, perhaps we can gather all our national sins into a single sanctum.**
