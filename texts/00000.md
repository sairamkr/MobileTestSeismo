








# **Speculations for an Unsettled Planet.**


*A collection of provocations by the students of Interaction Design at Anant National University, created as part of their Speculative Design course, Semester 06, 2026.*

The course introduced speculative design methods as a way to challenge normative practice and to examine the systemic inequities that are deepened by an unquestioning adherence to conventional practice.  
The works gathered here deliberately turn away from speculation as escapist futurism. Instead, the students have used their texts to interrogate power structures, surface hidden assumptions, and imagine alternative possibilities grounded in sustained critique. Throughout, the course placed particular emphasis on ethical practice within speculative design itself, attending to the risks of colonial speculation, exotification, and tech-solutionism.

**Taken together, these works seek to demonstrate that design has always served ideology, and to ask: who benefits from the precarious status quo of a fundamentally unsettled planet?**
