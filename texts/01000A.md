*Shravani & Harshada*

Welcome to a new way of eating. In a world where time is short and emotions are complicated, **the Symbiotic Fork** makes every meal simple again. No more guessing what you should feel when you taste something. No more pretending to enjoy a rare ingredient or a famous vintage. With this Fork, your emotions arrive instantly, perfectly matched to the food on your plate.

The secret is in the fork itself. **Each prong contains tiny bio-readers that scan the microbial signals present in your food. These signals are then translated into small, safe electrical pulses delivered directly to your vagus nerve, the powerful communication line between your gut and your brain.** The result? Immediate emotional clarity.

Insert the fork into a cake with a “happy” profile, and you might burst into laughter before you even swallow. Insert into something bold and “hostile,” and feel a focused surge of adrenaline or productive rage rush through your body. Eating becomes more than nourishment, it becomes a direct emotional interface.

We used to believe that eating was something we needed to be mindful about. People tried to listen to their bodies, interpret flavors, and understand their feelings. But let’s be honest, that's a lot of work. The Symbiotic Fork removes the burden completely. Your nervous system no longer has to struggle to interpret the world. The fork does that for you.

**Just pick up the fork , insert it in the food and feel exactly what the food intends.**

What controls our body? Could biology become a form of governance? What happens if food systems start regulating how we experience the world? And if technology continues to move closer to our bodies, where do we draw the line between assistance and control?

Soon, kitchens, restaurants, and dining tables everywhere will be built around emotional cuisine. Chefs won’t just design flavor, they will design emotional experiences. A menu might promise calm, excitement, nostalgia, or pure happiness.

And with the Symbiotic Fork, every promise is delivered instantly.

**By presenting a future that feels both ordinary and slightly unsettling, the Symbiotic Fork invites viewers to imagine how our relationship with everyday systems might change and what that might mean for the way we live.**
