Let's be honest: before we arrived, humanity was embarrassingly inefficient. For thousands of years our species stayed tied to the soil, spending its short life biting, grinding, and swallowing pieces of the earth just to survive. Messy, slow, and surprisingly primitive.

At ***Craving Cloud™*** we looked at the old "mouth-based" system and decided humanity deserved better. We didn't just improve food we moved beyond it. Why spend hours cooking, shopping, and cleaning in kitchens, those tiled monuments to wasted time, ***when nourishment can simply exist in the air around you?*** While governments spent decades debating food shortages, agriculture, and supply chains, we quietly replaced the whole system with something far simpler: ***breathing.***
